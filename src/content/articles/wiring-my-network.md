I spent a weekend last August running cable through the joists of an unfinished basement. By Sunday evening I had three VLANs, a working pfSense box, and a renewed respect for anyone who pulls cable for a living.

Networking is one of those domains where the abstractions are unusually good. You can spend years working above the wire and never need to know what a frame is. And then one day you do, and the abstractions get out of the way, and the machinery underneath turns out to be both beautiful and remarkably tolerant of amateurs.
