Loom is a small, opinionated reader for the open web. It does not recommend, it does not personalize, and it does not learn. It fetches what I tell it to fetch and shows it to me in the order it arrived.

I built it because every reader I tried eventually decided it knew better than I did. Loom does not know better. That is the feature.
